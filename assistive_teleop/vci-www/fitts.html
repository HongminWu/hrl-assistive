Fitt's Law Throughput Test

Instructions

This test measures your use of a virtual pointing device, such as a computer mouse.

Before you begin: Please maximize your browser window, so that the browser is as large as possible.

When the test begins you will see a red circle (a target).

Click on the target as quickly and accurately as possible.

When you click, a new target will be presented (even if you miss).

Click on each new target as quickly and accurately as possible.

There will be a few rounds with different size targets, different distances apart.

The complete test should take less than 5 minutes.

Once you've completed the test, click the link to download your data, and please e-mail the file to me.
