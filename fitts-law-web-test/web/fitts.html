Pointing Device Throughput Test

Instructions

1. If you use a dwell-click device, please enter the delay time (in seconds) here (otherwise leave as 0):

2. Please maximize your browser window, so that the browser is as large as possible.

3. Read through the following test overview:

  • When the test begins you will see a red circle (a target).
  • Try to click on the target as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • When you click, a new target will be presented (even if you miss).
  • Try to click on each new target as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • There will be multiple rounds with different size targets, different distances apart.
  • You may pause between rounds as long as you wish. Click the presented button to begin each round when you are ready.
  • For practice, you may take the test multiple times.
  • Once you've completed the test, your results will be sent to reserachers automatically.

4. When ready, click the button below. The test will begin immediately.
